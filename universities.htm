Knowledge Unlatched Research information for Institutions
 

Knowledge Unlatched Research involves researchers based at Curtin University’s Centre for Culture and Technology, Western Australia, in Queensland University of Technology’s IP and Innovation Law Program; and the University of Michigan Library in the US. We also work very closely with platforms, publishers, libraries and various research funders.

Our approach is highly collaborative: We recognize that our best chance of carrying out the high-impact, problem-focused research that is needed to support positive shifts in the scholarly communication landscape is to work with the best and brightest researchers, wherever they are located.

If you think that you might have something to add to understanding the challenges that we are interested in we would love to hear from you. If you have a suggestion about how we might work together to develop the knowledge, tools and resources needed to support open and networked digital futures for books, or would like to join our community, do please get in touch with us!
